Tuition and Fees

World Bridge is part of the International Schools Group (ISG), a not-for-profit organization of
independent schools, operating in Egypt for over thirty years. The not-for-profit model is the
gold standard in international education as 100% of its revenue is reinvested into the school.

2023-24 Tuition (in EGP, excluding VAT)

Grade/ Year Tuition Fee
PK / FS 1 20,000
KG1-2 / FS 2 - Year 1 30,000
Grade 1-5 / Year 2-6 40,000
Grade 6-8 / Year 7-9 50,000
Grade 9-12 / Year 10-13 60,000

The fees are divided into 3 installments:

Installment Due Date Amount
1st Installment July 1st 50%
2nd Installment October 1st 25%
3rd Installment January 1st 25%

Scholarship Programs

World Bridge offers a limited number of scholarships to students who demonstrate outstanding
academic achievement and potential. Scholarships are awarded to students who have demonstrated
excellence in academics, athletics, or the arts. Scholarships are awarded to students who have
demonstrated excellence in academics, athletics, or the arts.

Academic Scholarships

Academic scholarships are awarded to students who have demonstrated outstanding academic
achievement and potential. Students must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 to be considered for an
academic scholarship. Students must also submit a personal statement and a letter of
recommendation from a teacher or counselor.

Athletic Scholarships

Athletic scholarships are awarded to students who have demonstrated outstanding athletic
achievement and potential. Students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be considered for an
athletic scholarship. Students must also submit a personal statement and a letter of
recommendation from a coach or athletic director.

Arts Scholarships

Arts scholarships are awarded to students who have demonstrated outstanding achievement
and potential in the arts. Students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be considered for an
arts scholarship. Students must also submit a personal statement and a letter of
recommendation from an arts teacher or director.

Eligability

Students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be considered for a scholarship. Students must
also submit a personal statement and a letter of recommendation from a teacher or
counselor.

Financial Aid

World Bridge offers a limited number of financial aid packages to students who demonstrate
financial need. Financial aid packages are awarded to students who have demonstrated
financial need and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. Students must also submit a personal
statement and a letter of recommendation from a teacher or counselor.

Eligability

Students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be considered for a financial aid package. Students
must also submit a personal statement and a letter of recommendation from a teacher or
counselor.

Special cases fund program

  • Families with three (3) or more children at an ISG school are eligible for a discount on tuition.
  • The discount applies to the third child (and subsequent children) in the youngest grade(s) only.
  • The discount will be 20% of that child's tuition. If the installment plan is chosen, the discounted
    rate will be applied equally to all installment payments.
